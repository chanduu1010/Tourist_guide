Festivals & Traditions

Andhra Pradesh is renowned for its rich cultural heritage and vibrant traditions.

1. Ugadi

Ugadi marks the New Year for the Telugu people and is celebrated with great enthusiasm across the state.

2. Sankranti

Sankranti is the harvest festival, celebrated with kite flying, bonfires, and traditional sweet dishes.

3. Kuchipudi Dance

Kuchipudi is a classical Indian dance form that originated in Andhra Pradesh and is known for its graceful movements and intricate expressions.

4. Handloom Weaving

Andhra Pradesh is famous for its handloom weaving traditions, particularly Pochampally and Mangalgiri sarees.

5. Buddhist Heritage

Andhra Pradesh has a rich Buddhist history, with notable sites like Amaravati and Nagarjuna Konda reflecting ancient Buddhist heritage.
