Top Tourist Spots in Andhra Pradesh

Explore the diverse and stunning tourist destinations in Andhra Pradesh.

1. Tirupati

Tirupati is one of the most famous pilgrimage destinations in India, home to the renowned Sri Venkateswara Temple.

2. Visakhapatnam (Vizag)

Visakhapatnam, also known as Vizag, is a coastal city known for its scenic beaches, the INS Kursura Submarine Museum, and the beautiful Kailasagiri Hills.

3. Araku Valley

Located in the Eastern Ghats, Araku Valley is known for its coffee plantations, scenic beauty, and the Borra Caves, an ancient network of limestone caves.

4. Lepakshi Temple

The Lepakshi Temple is known for its rich Vijayanagara architecture and its gigantic Nandi (bull) statue, one of the largest in the country.

5. Srikalahasti

Srikalahasti is a popular religious site famous for its ancient Shiva temple, Srikalahasteeswara Temple, which is known for its architectural brilliance.

6. Gandikota

Often referred to as the "Grand Canyon of India," Gandikota is a stunning gorge on the banks of the Penna River with an ancient fort offering a breathtaking view.

7. Papi Hills

The Papi Hills are located along the Godavari River and offer a serene boat ride through the lush green hills, making it a perfect nature getaway.

8. Ahobilam

Ahobilam is a famous pilgrimage spot and a center of worship for Lord Narasimha. It is nestled in the Nallamala Hills, known for its caves and temples.

9. Konaseema

Konaseema is known for its scenic beauty, lush greenery, and coconut groves, situated in the delta region formed by the Godavari River.

10. Horsley Hills

Horsley Hills is a hill station known for its pleasant climate, panoramic views, and rich biodiversity, making it a favorite retreat for nature lovers.
